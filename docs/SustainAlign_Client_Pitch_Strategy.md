## 5. Regulatory Tailwinds — Why They Must Buy NOW

The MCA has made manual CSR management legally dangerous:

1. **CSR-1 is now web-only** (effective July 14, 2025). NGOs must register via MCA21 with DSC, CA/CS attestation, and real-time validation. Paper/PDF no longer accepted. [BuiltX, Aug 2025](https://www.builtxsdc.com/blog/new-csr-law-revised-2025)
2. **CSR-2 deadline was June 30, 2025** for FY24; companies face penalties for late filing. [Fintrac Advisors, Oct 2025](https://fintracadvisors.com/form-csr-2/)
3. **Unspent CSR Account transfers** are due within 30 days of FY-end (April 30). Miss it = penalty of ₹1 crore or 2x unspent amount. [Vinod Kothari, Apr 2025](https://vinodkothari.com/2025/04/ongoing-projects-unspent-funds-and-the-april-30-countdown/)
4. **Impact assessments** are mandatory for projects >₹1 crore and companies with CSR obligation >₹10 crore. [BuiltX, Sep 2025](https://www.builtxsdc.com/blog/top-10-csr-grants-in-india-2025)
5. **Social Stock Exchange (SSE)** is live on BSE/NSE but struggling. As of early 2026, only ~₹44 crore raised across <20 organizations. [The CSR Universe, May 2026](https://thecsruniverse.com/articles/can-india-s-social-stock-exchange-deliver-on-inclusive-finance-a-practitioner-s-view) Companies need better NGO/investor matchmaking — exactly SustainAlign's core.

**Translation for buyers:** "CSR compliance is no longer a Word document exercise. It is a digital audit trail. Buy now or face MCA scrutiny."
